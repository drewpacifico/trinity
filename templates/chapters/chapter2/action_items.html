Chapter 2

Action Items

Complete these tasks to apply your understanding of the industry landscape:

  • Identify 3-5 shipper types to target: Based on your local market, interests, and background, determine which customer types offer the best opportunities for you
  • Research major carriers in your region: Use FMCSA's database to identify asset-based carriers operating in your area. Note their equipment types and service areas
  • Study current capacity conditions: Check load boards, read industry publications, and talk to carriers to understand whether capacity is currently tight or loose in your primary markets
  • Choose 1-2 potential niches: Identify specializations that align with your background, local opportunities, and interests. Research the specific requirements of these niches
  • Subscribe to industry publications: Sign up for FreightWaves, Transport Topics, or other industry news sources to stay informed about market conditions and trends
  • Map your competitive landscape: List the brokerages, 3PLs, and other competitors serving your target market. Identify their strengths and how you'll differentiate yourself
  • Create a carrier research system: Set up a spreadsheet or CRM to track carriers you discover, including their equipment types, service areas, contact information, and notes on their capabilities
