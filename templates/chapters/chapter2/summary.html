How Money Flows

  • The chain: Customer pays you → You pay carrier → Margin is your gross profit
  • Your earnings: Commission split with brokerage (typically 50/50)
  • Key insight: Your income grows with volume AND margin—both matter

Market Dynamics

  • Tight capacity (more loads than trucks) = higher rates, easier to find freight, harder to find trucks
  • Loose capacity (more trucks than loads) = lower rates, harder to find freight, easier to find trucks
  • Seasonal patterns: Pre-holidays, harvest seasons, and month/quarter-end create tight capacityCapacityThe availability of trucks relative to loads needing transport; tight capacity (more loads than trucks) increases rates, loose capacity (more trucks than loads) decreases rates.; winter is typically slower
  • External factors: Economic conditions, weather events, regulatory changes, and fuel prices all affect rates

Your Competition

  • Direct competitors: Other agents, large brokerages, 3PLs, and digital freight platforms
  • Indirect competitors: Asset-based carriers with brokerage arms, direct shipper-carrier relationships
  • Your advantage: Personal service, specialized knowledge, and relationship-based trust
