Accessibility and Special Delivery Requirements

Various regulations and practical considerations affect delivery capabilities at different locations.

Liftgate Requirements

Liftgates are hydraulic platforms attached to trailers that raise and lower freight when locations lack loading docks.

  • Capacity limits: Standard liftgates handle 3,000-5,000 pounds depending on equipment. Heavy items may exceed liftgate capacityCapacityThe availability of trucks relative to loads needing transport; tight capacity (more loads than trucks) increases rates, loose capacity (more trucks than loads) decreases rates. requiring different solutions.
  • Regulations: OSHA requires liftgates meet safety standards including:
    • Load capacity ratings clearly marked
    • Safety mechanisms preventing accidental lowering
    • Proper maintenance and inspection
  • Costs: Liftgate service typically adds $50-100 to freight charges reflecting equipment costs and additional driver time.
⚠ Common Mistake
Always confirm delivery locations have docks or require liftgates when booking loads. Residential Delivery Restrictions

Residential deliveries present challenges leading many carriers to avoid them or charge premiums:

  • Limited maneuverability: Residential streets aren't designed for 53-foot trucks. Tight turns, low-hanging trees, and narrow roads create navigation difficulties.
  • Lack of loading equipment: Residences lack forklifts and loading docks. Manual unloading is time-consuming and increases injury risk.
  • Property damage risk: Driveways, lawns, and mailboxes can be damaged by large trucks.
  • Parking restrictions: Many residential areas prohibit or restrict commercial vehicle parking.
  • Signature requirements: Residential deliveries often require finding someone home to sign, creating scheduling complexity.

Residential deliveries typically carry $75-150 surcharges reflecting these challenges. Always confirm if delivery is residential when quoting and include appropriate charges.

Limited Access Locations

Various location types are considered "limited access" due to lack of professional receiving facilities:

  • Construction sites
  • Farms and agricultural facilities
  • Churches and places of worship
  • Schools and universities
  • Government facilities
  • Military bases
  • Prisons and correctional facilities
  • Mines and remote industrial sites
  • Self-storage facilities

Limited access locations typically carry $50-100 surcharges and may have additional restrictions (security clearances, appointment requirements, specific entry procedures).

Inside Delivery

When freight must be delivered beyond the truck tailgate—into a building, up stairs, or to a specific room—this is "inside delivery."

  • Not all carriers offer inside delivery
  • Costs typically $100-300+ depending on difficulty
  • May require additional labor or equipment
  • Confirm requirements and capabilities before booking

Appointment Requirements

Many receiving facilities require delivery appointments:

  • Appointments must be scheduled in advance
  • Missing appointments may result in fees or refusal
  • Some facilities have narrow windows (30-60 minutes)
  • Confirm appointment requirements during booking
💡 Pro Tip
Create a checklist for special delivery requirements: liftgate needed?, residential?, limited access?, inside delivery?, appointment required?, security procedures?, operating hours?. Ask these questions upfront to avoid surprises.

Practical application

Questions to ask when confirming delivery requirements:

  • "Does the delivery location have a loading dock?" If not, liftgate is required.
  • "Is this a residential address?" Residential deliveries carry surcharges and restrictions.
  • "Is inside delivery required?" Delivery beyond the truck tailgate costs extra.
  • "Does the receiver require an appointment?" Many facilities require scheduled deliveries.
  • "Are there any access restrictions or security procedures?" Limited access locations have special requirements.

Where to focus

For new agents, prioritize:

  • Ask upfront: Confirm delivery requirements when booking, not when the driver arrives.
  • Know the surcharges: Liftgate ($50-100), residential ($75-150), limited access ($50-100), inside delivery ($100-300+).
  • Create a checklist: Liftgate, residential, limited access, inside delivery, appointment, security procedures, operating hours.

Special delivery requirements affect pricing and carrier selection. Missing these details creates service failures and unexpected charges.
