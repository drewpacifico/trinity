Chapter 1

Action Items

Before moving to Chapter 2, complete these tasks to ensure you've absorbed the foundational concepts:

Task 1: Understand the Agent Role and Value Proposition

Write in your own words why a business would choose to work with you instead of calling carriers directly.

Task 2: Review Commission Structure with Your Brokerage

Confirm your specific commission split, payment terms, and any additional fees or requirements.

Task 3: Set Realistic Income Expectations for First Year

Create a personal financial plan showing how you'll cover expenses during the 3-6 month startup phase.

💡 Pro Tip
Take these action items seriously. They're designed to help you internalize the concepts and prepare you for the more technical content in the chapters ahead.
